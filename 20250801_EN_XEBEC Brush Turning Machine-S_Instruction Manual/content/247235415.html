Maximum Depth of Cut

Max depth of cut varies depending on the Brush angle.

Consider the burr root thickness when adjusting the depth of cut (burr root thickness must be 0.1 mm or less). Burr root thickness adds to the depth of cut, which may cause the maximum depth of cut to be exceeded, and the Brush breaking.

When using the product on an intermittent surface, the maximum depth of cut is 0.5 mm. The figure below shows examples of continuous and intermittent surfaces.
