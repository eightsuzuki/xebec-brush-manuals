How to Use

Fix the product on the machine and press the Brush against the rotating workpiece to remove burrs.
The Brush must be applied in the reverse feed direction, in the direction towards the end face of the Holder, regardless of whether it is applied for external turning or internal turning as shown in the figure below.

Applying this product in the forward feed direction may cause damages, resulting in operator injuries or loss of sight.
