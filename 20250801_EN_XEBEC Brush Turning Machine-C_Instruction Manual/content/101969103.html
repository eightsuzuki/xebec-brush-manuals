How to Assemble the Product

TM-SH-06
  1. Inset the Brush (A11-TB025) into the Holder.
  2. Insert the set screw (M2.6-6) into the hole at the tip of the Holder and tighten the screw using the hex key (torque: 0.25 Nm).


TM-SH-12
  1. Insert the Brush (A11/21/32-TB06) into the Holder.
  2. Insert the set screw (M2.6-10) into the hole at the side of the Holder and tighten the screw using the hex key (torque: 0.25 Nm).
